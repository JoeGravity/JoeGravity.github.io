About Me

Highly-skilled Graphic Designer, Illustrator and Web Designer/Developer, experienced in a wide variety of business and professional settings.

Full Stack Web developer with an emphasis in Front End UI/UX Design. Able to design, theme and build beautiful websites and applications, with full database backend, from mockup to rollout and beyond. Adept at implementing projects with diverse teams. Excited to continue learning new technologies and advancing my career in fresh directions.

I live in Minneapolis, have a wife, two kids, a dog and a bird. Plus more extended relatives than I can count. I enjoy fixing things, bicycling, building stuff with my kids, and traveling lakes and woods.

I've always liked to draw. Following that path, I studied at the University of Minnesota-Duluth and received my BFA in Graphic Design from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. For decades since then, I have been providing Graphic Design solutions for various businesses and organizations. I have a rich body of work, a friendly personality, and a vast knowledge of trivia.
